BOND CANCELLATION OF Advance LICENCE

Cancellation of Advance Licence:

  • The Advance Licence is issued to Manufacturer to import capital goods with 0% duty, but with conditions to fullfill the export obligation equivalent to 2 (Two years) of duty saved of imports made within validity period, licence validity is 2 (Two) years to complete Import as well as Export Obligation.
  • While Import is made under this Advance Licence, the license holder (Manufacturer) has to execute the Bond with Custom authorities under Notification no.96/2009 Dt.11/9/2009 specifying the liability of duty saved amount and fullfilment of export obligation within stipulated time period failing which the duty liability will be liable for recovery.
  • This executed Bond has to be got cancelled with customs authorities after producing the Export Obligation Discharge Certificate (EODC) issued by the licensing authorities.
  • The customs bond will remain active untill the EODC is not produced before them. The recovery process by customs will start on completion of licence validity.
